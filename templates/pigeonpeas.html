Production in India:

India is the leading producer of pigeon peas, contributing 4.34 million tonnes from an acreage of 5.05 million hectares with a productivity of 859 kg/ha. Uttar Pradesh is the top producer, followed by Madhya Pradesh, West Bengal, Bihar, and Jharkhand. Pigeon peas are majorly used as split cotyledons (dahl) and are an essential part of the vegan diet in the Indian subcontinent. India also leads in pigeon pea imports, accounting for 92.65% of global imports in 202111.

Market Trends:

The global pigeon pea market reached 13.3 million tons in 2022 and is projected to grow to 23.6 million tons by 2027 (CAGR of 9.51%). The estimated market value of pigeon pea in 2022 is US$ 14,334.5 million, expected to touch US$ 23,798 million by 203211.
