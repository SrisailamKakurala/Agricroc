Yield of Cantaloupe:

Yield depends mainly on crop management practices and variety cultivated. An average yield of 125 to 150 quintals per hectare can be obtained in musk melon cultivation.

Production Trends:

The cantaloupe seeds are sown from Nov to Dec in south India, Bihar & West Bengal. In plains, seeds are sown from Nov to Mar and in hills, seeds are sown from April to May.
