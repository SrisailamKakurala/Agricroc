Varieties:

India is the largest producer of mangoes globally, contributing over 45% of the worldâ€™s mango production. The country produces approximately 25 million metric tons of mangoes annually. There are about 1,000 varieties of mangoes in India, but only a few are commercially cultivated across the country. Cultivation Areas:

Mango cultivation thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. Major mango-growing states in India include: Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Bihar, Gujarat, and Telangana. The total mango cultivation area in India is 2,400 thousand hectares, with a production of 21.79 million MT2.

Export Potential:

India is a prominent exporter of fresh mangoes to the world. In the year 2022-23, India exported 22,963.76 MT of fresh mangoes worth Rs. 378.49 crores (approximately 48.53 USD millions). Major export destinations include the United Arab Emirates, U.K., USA, Qatar, and Kuwait2.
